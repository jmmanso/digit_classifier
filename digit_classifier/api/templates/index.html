Handwritten digit classifier

This is a REST service for recognition of digit images. Please upload a file, which needs to be less than 1 Mb in size and in png, jpeg, or gif format. The image resolution will be resampled to match the MNIST training dataset and, if rectangular, its shape will be cropped to a square.
